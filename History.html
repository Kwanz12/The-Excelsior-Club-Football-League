History

The History of Liga XCLR

What is The Excelsior Club Football League?

Liga XCLR Football League stands for Excelsior Club Football League. This league was mainly created to cater towards students on dorm. Dorm League was also created to help foster brotherhood, healthy competition and unity amongst peers who lived on Cedar Hall. This is where the Excelsior Club orginates. To dive into a bit of history...

The First Captains

*Cardinal's first ever team Captain was Quinell Ming, former President of the Excelsior Club

*Archer's first ever captain was Ivanhoe Smith, another former President of the Excelsior Club

* Ranger's first ever captain was Damanie Campbell

*Panther's first ever captain was Philip Graham (Currently the USM Sports Director of NCU)

* Astros is a new and recent team, and the've never won dorm league. Their first ever captain wa Jevaughn Patterson.

The Covid Season...

*Due to Covid-19, "Dorm League", had to be rebranded. It's rebranding started with only 2 teams due to Covid precautions, after 2 seasons, Rangers and Panthers Squads were created.

Personal All time records

*Ivanhoe Smith holds the all time goal scorer record

*Antoine Walker holds the all time assist record

*Lavoy Mitchell holds the all time clean sheet record

Squad all time records

*Archers has won the most dorm league titles 4

*Cardinals have won 2 league titles

*Rangers have won 2 league titles

*Panthers have never won any titles

*Astros have never won any league titles

*D.C.S a recently invited team who entered and won dorm league in the same year. Their Captains name was Tyreik Smith(Bull).

What's going on now?

In most recent years, during the 2024-2025 season, rangers have won the fall semester season, making them just one title behind archers, with the most titles in history.

The All time goal scorer record is still held by Ivanhoe Smith to this day, Wonder who will break it?

The all time assist record is still held by antoine walker, and the clean sheet record, has not been broken as yet.

Recently, we have seen a surge of talented players making their way foward to possibly breaking some records this season. During the 2024 Fall Semester Season, Jordan Brian Sterling, the Cardinals Captain for that season, scored 4 goals in a single season. Alongside Amos Brydon who scored 3 for his squad, the rangers team. Hats off to these Gentlmen. As we look to the future, youngsters such as Tajheim Henry and Jhavell Henry seem like the likliest prospects to perform in the 2025 Spring Semester Season.
